## Executive Summary

This photo index documents a comprehensive site inspection of **321 Humberline Drive, Etobicoke** - a 27,334 SF industrial manufacturing facility on 1.7 acres, built in 1978. The inspection was conducted over approximately 75 minutes (14:52 - 16:05) and captured 112 usable photographs plus supporting documentation. *(10 Avison Young valuation report photos were deleted 2026-01-15.)*

**Current Tenant/Owner-Occupant:** CAD-TEK Holdings Inc. (precision tool and die / metal stamping operation)

### Inspection Coverage

**Exterior Documentation (56 images):**
- **CN Rail Easement (EB387062):** Industrial lead track photographed along west/rear boundary, showing curved alignment through phragmites vegetation, at-grade crossing at Humberline Drive, ballast and tie condition (maintained but lightly used)
- **Building Elevations:** All four sides documented - concrete block/precast construction, brown fascia band, flat roof with parapet, 1970s industrial architecture
- **Front Facade:** CAD-TEK pylon signage at "321 Humberline Dr.", main office entrance with glass storefront, visitor parking
- **Parking Areas:** Extensive alligator cracking documented in employee parking - capital expenditure required for resurfacing
- **Yard Areas:** Metal recycling bins (Metalogics), rear yard unpaved with chain-link fencing, side yard between subject and 315 Humberline
- **Streetscape:** Humberline Drive (4-lane industrial arterial), rail crossing signage, truck traffic patterns
- **Neighboring Properties:** 315A/B Humberline (EFI/Prodigy 2-storey office), batch plant to south, new large-scale warehouse to west, KPMG/Polyair campus across street

**Interior Documentation (54 images):**
- **Office Component (~10% of building):** Reception area with CADOTEK signage, conference room (boardroom table, 6-8 seats, flat-screen TV), private offices with cherry wood furniture, file storage, design/engineering area with 3D printer
- **Manufacturing Floor:** Heavy stamping presses (DANLY 250-ton, Bliss brand, YSD), coil feed equipment, press brakes/shears, yellow safety guarding throughout
- **Tool & Die Shop:** "TOOL ROOM" signage, CNC lathe/turning center, vertical milling machines, surface grinders, jib cranes with chain hoists, precision workbenches with fixtures, workers actively present
- **CNC Machining:** TAKUMI V22A vertical machining center with automatic tool changer
- **Quality Control Lab:** Mitutoyo CMM (coordinate measuring machine), optical comparator/profile projector, climate-controlled separate from production
- **Electrical Infrastructure:** Main ITE switchgear, multiple sub-panels, 30 HP compressor disconnect, 30 kVA transformer, Toronto Hydro metering, substantial capacity for manufacturing operations
- **Employee Amenities:** Two break rooms with kitchenettes (refrigerators, microwaves, Keurig), employee lockers, washroom facilities, ceramic tile flooring
- **Shipping/Receiving:** Overhead sectional doors with air curtains, shipping office with schedule whiteboard
